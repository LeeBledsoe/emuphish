Welcome to Email Phishing Awareness

Email phishing is a type of cyber attack where criminals impersonate legitimate organizations to trick you into revealing sensitive information, such as passwords or credit card numbers. The goal of this site is to help you understand what phishing is, recognize its signs, and learn how to protect yourself.

Explore our resources to learn more about different types of phishing attacks and how to identify them. Test your knowledge through our interactive quiz to see how well you can spot a phishing attempt.
